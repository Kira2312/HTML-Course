Facts about chess

1. The longest possible chess game is 5,949 moves! 2. "Checkmate" comes from the Persian "Shah Mat," meaning "the king is helpless." 3. The second book ever printed in English was about chess. 4. There are more possible chess games than atoms in the observable universe. 5. The youngest grandmaster ever was 12 years old — Abhimanyu Mishra.
